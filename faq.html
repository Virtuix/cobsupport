Parkour and Hanghout.

Are there in-game chat features for communication with other players?

Yes. You can communicate with other players using Safe Chat. Click on your character and type digit of message that you want to send./p>

How does matchmaking work? Is it skill-based or random?

In our game you don't have to worry that someone is faster. Everyone plays at their own pace and the only thing that counts is fun!

Are there penalties for leaving a multiplayer game early?

No. If you give up, nothing will happen to you.

Can I customize my character or profile in the multiplayer mode?

Currently, it is not possible to select a username or change the character's color and it will probably not be possible in the future.

What happens if there are connection issues during a multiplayer?

If there are connection issues, there will only be sync issues with other players. This won't stop you from completing the level.

Are there any rewards or achievements specific to multiplayer gameplay?

After completing the level, you will be able to continue playing while watching other players or looking for secret locations.

Is there a reporting system for inappropriate behavior or cheating?

By creating Safe Chat, we ensured security in communication between users. In case of cheating, please use the form located [here] or in the footer of the page.

How are server outages or maintenance periods handled?

We do not take breaks while repairing game servers or the website. Everything is checked for errors before publication. This keeps players constantly engaged in the game.

Can I play cross-platform with users on different devices?

Sure!

Are there scheduled events or tournaments for multiplayer competitions?

the main goal of the game is the so-called "speedrun", i.e. completing the game as quickly as possible. We will organize competitions based on this.

How are updates and patches managed for the multiplayer aspect of the game?

We change game files on the server. During gameplay, you don't will lose connection, but subsequent players will be automatically transferred to the newer version of the game.
